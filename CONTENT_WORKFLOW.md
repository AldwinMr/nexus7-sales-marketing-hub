# Content Workflow

## Status Flow

Draft → Review → Approved → Published → Archived

## Rules

- Draft content should stay in working branches.
- Approved content must be reviewed before merge.
- Video generation should only run from approved briefs.
- Generated videos must go to `04-video/generated/pending-review/`.
- Final approved videos go to `04-video/generated/approved/`.

## Required Review

Content must be reviewed for:

- Accuracy
- Brand alignment
- Clear call to action
- Correct target audience
- No misleading claims
- No unapproved pricing or offers
