# Approval Rules

## Who Can Create Content

Sales and marketing contributors may create:

- Campaign briefs
- Social posts
- Video briefs
- Sales scripts
- Landing page copy
- Objection handling notes

## Who Can Approve

Only the repository owner or assigned approver may approve:

- Final campaign copy
- Sales offers
- Pricing language
- Generated videos
- Public-facing landing page copy

## Automation Rule

Video automation should only run when content is marked approved.
